Egyptian Museum

  • The Egyptian Museum of Barcelona has a collection made up of more than 1,100 pieces from Ancient Egypt. In this museum they have guided tours and educational services

  • Many of the domestic objects we use today were invented or used by the ancient Egyptians, among them are the hair brushes, scissors, wigs, makeup, etc ... The clothes they used were light so the material chosen was the lli, which in they considered it pure.

  • Theyr jewlery was large, and heavy. The clothes of someone depended on the social class to which you were part of. The mummified bodies were placed in special tombs within the pyramids, however, ordinary citizens were put in the sand.

  • The general admission is € 12.00 or € 8.50 for students and € 5.00 for children. Address: Carrer de València, 284, 08007 Barcelona Accessible with metro, car and bus.

    • Villa del Art Museum

  • It is one of the main galleries of contemporary art in Catalonia. It was founded in Barcelona in 1999. There are more than 30 artists who show their art, including painters, photographers and national and international sculptors. They also exhibit art from less-known artists, without giving less importance to any unfamous artist

  • Address: Carrer de la Tapineria, 39, 08002 Barcelona
